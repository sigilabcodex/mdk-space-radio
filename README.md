# MDK Space Radio

24/7 experimental electronic transmission from the MDK archive.

MDK is Mik Schuppin, Diego Madero, and Kai Kraatz: three humans improvising electronic music remotely between Germany and Mexico.

The radio streams public MDK releases hosted on the Internet Archive.

Music by MDK is published under CC BY 3.0 unless otherwise noted.

## Public radio

https://radio.mdkband.com/

## Catalog

The canonical public radio catalog lives at:

`catalog/radio-manifest.json`

The VPS radio consumes this manifest to generate the live playlist.
